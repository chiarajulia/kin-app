What first-time investors invest in 📊
Based on common practice among new investors in Europe. Not financial advice.
🌍
MSCI World ETF
Most common
Tracks ~1,500 companies across 23 developed countries. One fund, maximum diversification. Popular options: iShares Core MSCI World, Vanguard FTSE All-World.
🇪🇺
S&P 500 ETF
Very popular
Tracks the 500 largest US companies. Higher growth potential, higher concentration in one market.
💰
High-yield savings account
Safety first
For the safety bucket goal — not investing, but a guaranteed return (currently 2–3.5% in Europe) with no risk.
🏢
ESG / Sustainable ETFs
Growing fast
ETFs that screen for environmental, social and governance criteria. Slightly more expensive but aligns money with values.
⚠️ KIN does not give financial advice. The above reflects common practice among new investors and is for educational purposes only. Always do your own research or consult a licensed financial advisor.
