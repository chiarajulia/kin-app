What first-time investors choose
Common practice among new investors in Europe. Not financial advice.
MSCI World ETFMost common
Tracks ~1,500 companies across 23 developed countries. One fund, broad diversification. Popular options: iShares Core MSCI World, Vanguard FTSE All-World.
S&P 500 ETFVery popular
Tracks the 500 largest US companies. Higher historical growth, higher concentration in one market.
High-yield savingsSafety first
For the safety bucket goal — guaranteed return (2–3.5% in Europe) with no market risk.
ESG / Sustainable ETFsGrowing
Screens for environmental, social and governance criteria. Slightly higher cost but aligns money with values.
KIN does not give financial advice. The above reflects common practice among new investors and is for educational purposes only. Always do your own research or consult a licensed financial advisor.
